Online starters courses of 3 hours:

  1. Introductory     course in identification of seeds and fruits for seed companies

Target audience: laboratory technicians and analysts in seed companies, plant breeding companies and performers of seed germination and purification tests

  1. Introductory     course in the determination of seeds and fruits for post docs and     international exchange students.

Target audience: post docs and exchange students with training in plant biology, botanical archaeology and plant breeding.

Basic training: four modules of each 1 day comprising nine plant families: Fabaceae, Brassicaceae, Polygonaceae, Asteraceae, Poaceae, Cyperaceae, Apiaceae, Boraginaceae & Malvaceae.
